How It Works

Getting started with FoodShare is simple and easy for everyone.

1

Sign Up

Create an account as a donator, recipient, or orphanage.

2

List or Browse

Donators list available food, others browse what's available.

3

Connect

Arrange pickup or delivery of the food items.

4

Share

Complete the share and help reduce food waste!
